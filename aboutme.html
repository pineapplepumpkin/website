    my design philosophy

    I would like my work to be guided by a commitment to inclusivity and accessibility. In the most ideal sense, I think being a good designer comes from empathy driven problem solving. In a practical sense, it may not always be possible - trying to find the best balance of user focused problem solving and business feasibility is the most realistic. Projects that focus on accessibility, inclusivity and harm mitigation would be my priority.

    I’m committed to continuous growth and delivering work that meets the highest standards. Whether zooming out to align with broader strategies or diving deep into the nuances, I would approach every project holistically and cross-functionally. I’d like my work to connect to a deeper purpose—improving lives and leaving the world better than I found it.
